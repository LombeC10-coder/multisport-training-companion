# MultiSport — Training Companion

MultiSport is a mobile performance companion for serious low-tier and off-season athletes who do not have access to elite-level coaching, physio, or strength and conditioning support.

The app helps athletes structure their training, track workload, monitor recovery, and make safer training decisions using simple logging, RPE-based load scoring, recovery check-ins, and injury-aware guardrails.

## Core Promise

Train like you have a performance team, even when you do not.

## Target Users

- Low-tier competitive athletes
- Off-season athletes training alone
- University athletes
- Semi-pro and amateur players
- Gym and sport hybrids
- Athletes without regular access to physios, S&C coaches, or structured performance teams

## MVP Features

- Athlete profile setup
- Sport and training goal selection
- Session templates
- Quick session logging
- RPE-based training load calculation
- Weekly load dashboard
- Recovery check-ins
- Pain and injury-aware safety mode

## Training Load Formula

Training load is calculated using duration multiplied by RPE.

Example:

60 minutes x RPE 8 = 480 load

## MVP Goal

The MVP goal is to let an athlete:

1. Create a profile
2. Select sports and training goals
3. Log training sessions
4. Track weekly load
5. Complete recovery check-ins
6. Record pain areas and pain score
7. Receive simple safety recommendations
